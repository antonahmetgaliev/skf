1. General Information

  1. This regulation may be amended or supplemented by the Responsible Officials, taking into account the wishes and comments of the drivers during breaks between championships.
  2. No changes to the regulation are allowed during the championship. The SKF Racing Hub NASCAR General Regulations apply, which every participant must read and comply with.

2. Championship Organization

  1. The championship (hereinafter — the Championship) is held on the iRacing.com Race Simulation simulator (PC version) using the governing documents (Individual Regulations and Driving Rules). The panel of stewards consists of Andrii Shumarin, Dmytro Kotsiuk and Kyrylo Taran.
  2. The official championship announcement board is maintained:
    • on the organizer’s Discord;
    • on SKF Racing Hub social media.
  3. Current championship information is published on all official SKF Racing Hub resources.
  4. All drivers and responsible persons must follow impartial reporting rules and must not distort race results.
  5. Disputes are resolved through submission of protests.
  6. Championship stewarding is conducted offline, with all incidents and protests reviewed.
  7. The protest window will remain open for 24 hours after the race and will be available on this website — skfracinghub.com/incidents.

3. Championship

  1. Each championship round is conducted according to the approved calendar in the individual standings.
  2. The championship consists of eight rounds.
  3. Only the results of the seven best rounds for each driver are counted in the final individual standings.
  4. The date and track for each round are specified in the calendar, which is prepared by the Organizer and published on the Organizer’s Discord in the #iracing-2026-calendar channel and on social media.

Race Rules

  • Fast Repairs: 1
  • Setup: Fixed
  • Additional tyres: 3
  • DT Penalties: 17 incident points
  • Disqualification: 25 incident points
  • Green / White / Checkered: 15
  • Automatic Full Course Yellow: on (except Round 1)
  • Lucky Dog: on (except Round 1)
  • Assistants: Disabled (but Clutch Assist OK)
  • Start: Rolling

Race Schedule

  • 18:00 CEST — Practice (60 min)
  • 19:00 CEST — Qualifying (5 min | 2 laps)
  • 19:05+ CEST — Race
  • 20:05+ CEST — Post-race wrap-up
  • 20:45+ CEST — Publication of preliminary results
  1. Round dates and times may be changed by the Race Directorate, with participants notified at least 48 hours in advance.
  2. Preliminary results are published on TheSimGrid.

4. Participation in the Championship

  1. Participants must meet the following requirements:
    • Minimum age 16 (in special cases, age may be lowered after an interview and skill assessment)
    • Stable internet connection
    • Ownership of iRacing.com Race Simulation
    • Availability of the championship cars and tracks
    • Only steering wheels and gamepads are allowed as controls
  2. Using a keyboard to control the car is prohibited.
  3. Maximum number of participants: 30 drivers.
  4. Drivers must use their real first and last names in Latin letters, matching their iRacing registration.
  5. Registration and participation under multiple names are prohibited.
  6. Participants are fully responsible for their actions, inactions, and the equipment used.
  7. Drivers must follow all instructions from the Organizer during the championship.
  8. Drivers may use driving assists allowed by the server settings and adjust them if permitted by the simulator.
  9. Drivers have the right to submit protests.
  10. In-game driver rating affects participation and is considered during registration.
  11. Drivers must compete in the car declared during registration.
  12. Using an undeclared car will result in disqualification.
  13. A round officially starts when a driver reaches their starting position.

5. Driver Registration

  1. Registration links are published on all SKF Racing Hub resources.
  2. The list of championship participants is available in the corresponding section on TheSimGrid.
  3. Drivers wishing to participate must fill out an application form. Applications are submitted via TheSimGrid.
  4. Entry fee: 0 UAH (free of charge).
  5. All participants must join the SKF Racing Hub Discord.

6. Cars and Classes

  1. The championship uses the NASCAR Trucks.
  2. Drivers may use custom liveries, which can be changed during the championship, but changing the starting number is prohibited.
  3. Drivers are responsible for the content of their custom liveries. In case of disputes or legal issues, the Organizer bears no responsibility.
  4. Changing the car during the championship is not permitted. Each driver must use the car selected at registration for the entire season.

7. Scoring

  1. After each championship race, the following classification is established: individual driver standings.
  2. Drivers receive points in the individual standings according to their finishing position:
    Pos 12345 678910 1112131415 1617181920 2122232425 2627282930
    Pts 3529282726 2524232221 2019181716 1514131211 109876 54321
  3. Points are awarded after stewards check and approve all drivers’ results, including any penalties.
  4. Drivers with DNF/DSQ (Did Not Finish / Disqualified) receive no points.
  5. If two or more drivers have equal points, the driver with more first-place finishes ranks higher. If first-place finishes are equal, second places are considered, and so on.
  6. If points and positions remain equal, the driver with the better qualifying result (starting from Round 1) ranks higher.

10. Championship Standings & Awards

  1. Championship positions are determined by the total points scored by drivers in their seven best rounds.
  2. If fewer than half of the rounds in the calendar are completed, the championship is considered invalid.
  3. Winners in the SKF Thunder Trucks Series Season 2 individual standings are awarded the following titles:
    • 1st place: “Champion SKF Thunder Trucks Series Season 2”
    • 2nd place: “Silver Medalist SKF Thunder Trucks Series Season 2”
    • 3rd place: “Bronze Medalist SKF Thunder Trucks Series Season 2”
