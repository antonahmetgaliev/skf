Who We Are

Born from the Love of Racing

SKF Racing Hub is a community of sim racers who race together week after week across the world's greatest circuits. We run structured championships with real stewarding, race-by-race standings, and a licence system that rewards clean, competitive driving.

Whether you're a seasoned driver pushing for the title or a newcomer looking for your first wheel-to-wheel battles — there's a seat for you.
